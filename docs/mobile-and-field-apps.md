# Mobile & Field Applications

The NovaEco ecosystem bridges the digital cloud and the physical world. While **NovaAdmin** provides a desktop "Mission Control" for planners and executives, physical execution happens on the ground. 

We provide specialized, progressive web apps (PWAs) and native mobile interfaces tailored to specific field roles.

## 1. The Citizen App (NovaPack & NovaRetail)
Designed for everyday consumers participating in the circular economy.
* **Deposit Returns:** Scan QR codes at smart bins or Reverse Vending Machines to instantly receive `NovaFin` deposit refunds.
* **Digital Product Passports (DPP):** Scan NFC tags or QR codes on clothing (`NovaTextile`) or electronics (`NovaTronix`) to view material provenance, repair guides, and recyclability scores.

## 2. The Field Technician App (NovaInfra & NovaSkills)
Designed for IoT installers and repair technicians.
* **Sensor Provisioning:** Technicians can securely enroll new hardware (e.g., a `NovaWater` pH sensor) into the ecosystem simply by scanning its secure QR code.
* **Verifiable Credentials:** Workers store their cryptographic certifications in the `NovaSkills` wallet, proving they are qualified to perform hazardous repairs (e.g., swapping high-voltage batteries).

## 3. The Fleet Driver App (NovaLogistics)
Designed for reverse-logistics operators and waste haulers.
* **Dynamic Routing:** Drivers receive real-time, optimized routes based on smart-bin fill levels (`NovaRecycle`) rather than static weekly schedules.
* **Digital Waste Transfer Notes (dWTN):** Drivers can cryptographically sign custody handovers for hazardous materials directly on their mobile device, satisfying `NovaPolicy` compliance automatically.
