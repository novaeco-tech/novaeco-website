We are proud to announce that the **NovaEco V2 Architecture** is officially entering its next phase of active development! After rigorous architectural planning and migrating to a high-performance, event-driven polyrepo structure, we are opening our digital doors to the community.

NovaEco is designed to bridge the gap between isolated industrial sectors and a truly collaborative, planetary-scale circular economy.

## What is NovaEco?

NovaEco is an open-source, institutional-grade Digital Public Infrastructure (DPI). Whether you are a city planner mapping municipal waste streams with **UrbanMiner**, or a developer building automated Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) calculators, we provide the digital plumbing you need to succeed. 

Our federated "System-of-Systems" allows independent sectors to interact securely without falling into the trap of monolithic, centralized silos.

### Key Features of V2

* **Federated Orchestration:** Connect disparate industries using our 11+ shared **Horizontal Enablers** (like `NovaFin` and `NovaTrade`) and 13+ specialized **Vertical Sectors** (like `NovaAgro` and `NovaMake`).
* **Zero-Trust Security:** Our split-face identity mesh uses `novaeco-auth` for high-speed gRPC token validation, ensuring your internal telemetry streams and external API requests remain strictly authenticated.
* **Automated Environmental Auditing:** Validate your impact dynamically with `NovaBalance` LCA workers, and enforce global regulatory compliance (like REACH and CBAM) autonomously using `NovaPolicy`.
